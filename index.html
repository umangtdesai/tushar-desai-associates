We're

Designs that impact, designs that work.

Tushar Desai Associates is an architecture and interior design firm based in Mumbai, India.

We have focused on creating high calibre architectural & interior design solutions that are unique yet unpretentious. Our winning of National Award from IIID, the Apex body of Interior Designers in India, for 3 consecutive years, each for a different category validate our ‘bold’ approach to design & our spirited endeavour in taking an uncharted course. We constantly look forward to variety and new challenges in our work..

Our Design Approach is uniquely tailored to our client's needs.
