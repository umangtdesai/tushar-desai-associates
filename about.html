About the Company




History:

Over last 33 years, Tushar Desai Associates have focused on creating high calibre architectural & interior design solutions that are unique yet unpretentious. Today Tushar Desai is listed one of the TOP 50 Interior Designers in the Industry.

From 2010 to 2014, he was the Vice President of Institute of Indian Interior Designers, the apex body of interior design with 30 chapters and over 8,000 members and was also the chairman of the Awards Committee 2006-2014 and has steered the awards programme to becoming the most sought after Awards in the field.

Our Values:

The design must create a sustainable environment that relaxes while creating memorable experiences. We strive to practice high levels of management & professional standards to deliver excellence in design, while adhering to time & budgetary parameters.

TDA offers comprehensive services of International standards in: Architecture, Interior Design, Master planning and Landscaping in numerous industries like hospitality, educational, corporate, residential, and healthcare.
